About the Society

The Junior Philippine Computer Society (JPCS) is the student arm of the Philippine Computer Society, the oldest IT professional organization in the country.

Our Mission

To provide a venue for students to grow technically and professionally through collaborative projects and industry exposure.

Our Vision

To be the leading student organization in the Philippines producing globally competitive IT practitioners.
